AutoWhisk: Ergonomic Matcha Whisk Redesign
Traditional matcha whisking can strain the wrist through repeated motion and awkward grip angles. Our team developed AutoWhisk, an ergonomic solution that automates the whisking process. The device attaches to a lid and features a rotator with customizable speed, duration, and whisking style—allowing users to prepare matcha hands-free while minimizing physical stress. The design integrates physical prototyping, user testing, and HCI principles.
